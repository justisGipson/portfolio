About Me

After spending the last 6 years as an artist and instructor working with glass at the Indianapolis Art Center, I quickly discovered I absolutely love the challenge of programming. At that time I decided to start teaching myself. I started off using codecademy.com to learn python and sql and used those courses to complete the Data Science learning path they offer, which includes several sections on machine learning. At that time I was also using freecodecamp.org and sololearn.com to teach myself HTML and CSS. Soon after I learned git and started utilizing GitHub to store my projects. I have also become comfortable using terminal to navigate my machine. At the beginning of October 2018 I found out I was accepted to attend the 12-week JavaScript boot-camp at Eleven Fifty Academy in Fishers, Indiana from where I am planning to graduate in March of 2019. Upon finding out I had been accepted I started teaching myself JavaScript fulltime until the start of class at the beginning of December 2018. In my free time I like to tinker with my Raspberry Pi at home. I am also an avid backpacker, traveller and amateur nature photographer. My wife and I have driven over 30,000 miles over the past few years to visit National Parks and to hike and camp in some of the most amazing places in the lower 48.
